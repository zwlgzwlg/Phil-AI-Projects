Rules

  • Black gets a free stone in the centre.
  • White plays first, placing 2 stones.
  • Players then alternate placing 2 stones each turn.
  • First to get 6 in a row wins.
  • Lines can run along any of the 3 hex axes.
  • The board wraps around (torus).
